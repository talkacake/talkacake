4. Grammar Rules Recap 📏
สรุปกฎไวยากรณ์ (Do / Does Questions)

👥 DO (พหูพจน์ + I/You)

I, You, We, They
(หรือประธานที่มี 2 คน/สิ่งขึ้นไป)

"Do you like movies?"
"Do they eat meat?"

👤 DOES (เอกพจน์)

He, She, It
(หรือประธานที่มี 1 คน/สิ่ง)

"Does she have a cat?"
"Does the alien fly?"

⚠️ Important Rule! (กฎสำคัญ)

After Does, the verb goes back to normal! No "S"!
(หลัง Does คำกริยาจะกลับเป็นรูปปกติ ไม่เติม s)

❌ Does he likes apples?
✅ Does he like apples?

5. Exercise Time! 📝
เวลาทำแบบฝึกหัด!

A. Choose Do or Does (เลือก Do หรือ Does)

1. ________ you have a lot of clothes? 👗
Correct! "You" takes Do.
2. ________ your father work at a hospital? 🏥
Correct! "Your father" is He (Singular) -> Does.
3. What time ________ the movie start? 🍿
Correct! "The movie" is It (Singular) -> does.
4. How much water ________ they drink per day? 💧
Correct! "They" takes do.
5. ________ your friends like playing soccer? ⚽
Correct! "Your friends" is They (Plural) -> Do.

B. Error Correction 🔧 (แก้ไขจุดที่ผิด)

❌ 1. Do she want to be an actor?
✅ Does she want to be an actor?
(She -> Does)
❌ 2. Does he likes skinny jeans?
✅ Does he like skinny jeans?
(After Does, NO 's'!)
❌ 3. Do they has a lot of money?
✅ Do they have a lot of money?
(They -> Do, and base word 'have')
❌ 4. Does they eat pizza?
✅ Do they eat pizza?
(They -> Do)
❌ 5. Do the dog bark at night?
✅ Does the dog bark at night?
(The dog = It -> Does)

C. Fill in the blanks (Gap-fill) ✍️ (เติมคำในช่องว่าง do หรือ does)

1. you want a fortune cookie? 🥠
2. Why the dog bark so much? 🐕
3. How much time we have left? ⏳
4. Where she buy raw meat? 🥩
5. they sleep in the wardrobe? 🚪

D. Jumbled Sentences 🧩 (เรียงประโยคให้ถูกต้อง)

1. [ pizza / he / Does / like / ? ]
✅ Does he like pizza? 🍕
2. [ they / go / to / Do / the mall / ? ]
✅ Do they go to the mall? 🏬
3. [ you / Do / have / a pen / ? ]
✅ Do you have a pen? 🖊️
4. [ work / she / Does / here / ? ]
✅ Does she work here? 🏢
5. [ know / they / the answer / Do / ? ]
✅ Do they know the answer? 💡
