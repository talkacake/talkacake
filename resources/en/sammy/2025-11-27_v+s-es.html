2. The "S" Rules (He / She / It) 📏 กฎการเติม S (สำหรับ เขา/เธอ/มัน)

I / You / We / They
Run / Eat / Go
(No S) ❌
He / She / It
Runs / Eats / Goes
(Add S or ES) ✅

Special Rule: Words ending in -o, -ch, -sh add -es. คำที่ลงท้ายด้วย o, ch, sh ให้เติม es

  • Go ➡️ Goes
  • Watch ➡️ Watches
  • Brush ➡️ Brushes

3. Fix the Verb! 🛠️ แก้คำกริยาให้ถูกต้อง!

Say the correct sentence, then click to check.

She / usually / (eat) / breakfast.
She usually eats breakfast.
He / often / (watch) / TV.
He often watches TV. (End with -ch!)
They / never / (sleep) / in class.
They never sleep in class. (No S! "They")
My mom / always / (cook) / dinner.
My mom always cooks dinner. (Mom = She)
He / usually / (go) / to bed late.
He usually goes to bed late. (End with -o!)

4. Interview: Talking about Others 🎤 สัมภาษณ์: พูดถึงคนอื่น (บังคับใช้ S)

Teacher: Ask me about my best friend, "Tom".

Ask me: (play tennis?)

👉 Does he play tennis?

Teacher Answers: Yes, he ________ (play) tennis every Sunday.

He plays tennis every Sunday.

Teacher: Ask me about my sister, "Lisa".

Ask me: (go to the gym?)

👉 Does she go to the gym?

Teacher Answers: Sometimes. She ________ (go) on Mondays.

She goes on Mondays.

5. Battle: Routine vs. Now ⚔️ ประลอง: กิจวัตร vs ตอนนี้

📅 Routine (Simple)
Every day / Usually / Often
👉 eats / plays
👀 Now (Continuous)
Look! / Right now / Listen!
👉 is eating / is playing

Click the correct verb!

1. Look! He ________ in class!

sleeps is sleeping

2. She ________ dinner every evening.

cooks is cooking

3. I ________ TV right now.

am watching watch

4. They usually ________ to the gym on Sundays.

are going go

5. Listen! The baby ________.

is crying cries

6. Extension Activities 🚀 กิจกรรมเสริม

🤖 Game: My Dream Robot เกม: หุ่นยนต์ในฝันของฉัน

Describe what your robot does for you. Use "It" + Verb(s/es).

1. (clean / my room) 👉 It cleans my room.

2. (do / my homework) 👉 It ________ my homework.

It does my homework.

3. (wash / my car) 👉 It ________ my car.

It washes my car.

4. (cook / dinner) 👉 It ________ dinner.

It cooks dinner.

🕵️‍♀️ Role Play: The Detective บทบาทสมมติ: นักสืบ

Teacher: I am a suspect. Ask me about my day!

Student: What time / you / get up? (Ask about "he")

👉 What time does he get up?

Suspect: He gets up at 10 AM.

Student: What / he / eat / for lunch?

👉 What does he eat for lunch?
