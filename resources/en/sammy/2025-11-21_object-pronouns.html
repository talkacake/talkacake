Lesson 9: Me, Him, Her, Us

Object Pronouns

Speed Review!

Challenge: Answer quickly with a FULL SENTENCE.

(ความท้าทาย: ตอบเร็วๆ ด้วย **ประโยคเต็ม** ห้ามตอบสั้นๆ)

Q1: Is there a TV in your room?
Try to say: "Yes, there is..." or "No, there isn't..."
"Yes, there is a TV in my room."
"No, there isn't a TV in my room."
Q2: What are you doing now?
Try to say: "I am..."
"I am studying English."
Q3: What are you going to do tomorrow?
Try to say: "I am going to..."
"I am going to eat pizza."

(Or whatever you will do!)

Why use pronouns? (ทำไมต้องใช้?)

❌ Too many names (ชื่อเยอะเกินไป)

"I love Tom."

"I call Tom."

"I give money to Tom."

✅ Better (ดีกว่า)

"I love Tom."

"I call him."

"I give money to him."

The Rules (กฎ)

Use these words after the verb (ใช้คำเหล่านี้ หลัง คำกริยา).

I (ฉัน - ประธาน) ➡️
me (ฉัน - กรรม)
He (เขา) ➡️
him (เขา)
She (เธอ) ➡️
her (เธอ)
It (มัน) ➡️
it (มัน)
They (พวกเขา) ➡️
them (พวกเขา)
We (พวกเรา) ➡️
us (พวกเรา)
