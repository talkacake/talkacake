Grammar Detective 🕵️‍♂️🔍 นักสืบไวยากรณ์ (หาจุดผิดและแก้ไข)

Your mission: Find the mistakes and fix them!
(ภารกิจของคุณ: หาจุดผิดและแก้ไขมันให้ถูกต้อง!)

1. Detective Training (Warm Up) 🧠 ฝึกฝนนักสืบ (อุ่นเครื่อง)

Read the bad sentence. Speak the correct sentence! (อ่านประโยคที่ผิด แล้วพูดประโยคที่ถูก!)

👽 Alien says: "I is a student."
You say: "I am a student." (Is/Am/Are)
👽 Alien says: "He go to the mall every day."
You say: "He goes to the mall every day." (Present Simple -s/es)
👽 Alien says: "Yesterday, I am at the cinema."
You say: "Yesterday, I was at the cinema." (Past Simple was/were)
👽 Alien says: "How much apples do you want?"
You say: "How many apples do you want?" (Countable nouns)

2. Case File: The Hungry Ghost 👻🍔 แฟ้มคดี: ผีจอมหิว

Be the detective! Correct the ghost's bad grammar. (เป็นนักสืบ! แก้แกรมม่าของผีให้ถูกต้อง)

Ghost: Hello. I is I am a ghost.

Ghost: I want to order food. Do you have some waters? any water?

Ghost: I want to eat two childs. two children.

Detective (You): No! You cannot eat children! Do you want this sandwiches? these sandwiches?

Ghost: Okay. Yesterday, I eat ate a sandwich and it were was good!

4. Speaking Challenge (Pair Work) 🗣️ ความท้าทายในการพูด

Teacher reads the BAD sentence. Student must INTERRUPT and FIX it immediately!

❌ Teacher: She going to order a wonton soup.
✅ Student: Wait! She is going to order a wonton soup! (Future Simple)

❌ Teacher: There is two pillows on the bed.
✅ Student: Wait! There are two pillows on the bed! (There is/are)

❌ Teacher: I don't like he.
✅ Student: Wait! I don't like him! (Object Pronouns)

❌ Teacher: Does you have any vegetables?
✅ Student: Wait! Do you have any vegetables? (Do/Does)

❌ Teacher: I am a owl.
✅ Student: Wait! I am an owl! (Articles)

5. Extension: Extra Detective Work! ⏱️🔍 กิจกรรมเสริม: งานนักสืบพิเศษ! (เผื่อเวลาเหลือ)

Finished early? Let's play more games! เสร็จเร็วเหรอ? มาเล่นเกมเพิ่มกันเถอะ!

A. Role Play: The Broken Robot 🤖💥 บทบาทสมมติ: หุ่นยนต์พัง

Rule: The teacher is a robot. Ask the robot questions. The robot will answer with BAD grammar. Fix the robot! กฎ: ครูเป็นหุ่นยนต์ ให้นักเรียนถามคำถาม หุ่นยนต์จะตอบผิดแกรมม่า ให้นักเรียนแก้ให้ถูก!

🗣️ You ask: What are you doing?

🤖 Robot: I is talking.

🗣️ You fix: No! You are talking. (Or: I am talking)

🗣️ You ask: Do you like apples?

🤖 Robot: Yes, I likes apples.

🗣️ You fix: No! I like apples. (No 's')

B. Listening Game: STOP! 🛑👂 เกมการฟัง: หยุดนะ!

Rule: The teacher reads a story. When you hear bad grammar, shout "STOP!" and fix it. กฎ: ครูเล่าเรื่อง เมื่อคุณได้ยินแกรมม่าที่ผิด ให้ตะโกนว่า "หยุด!" แล้วแก้ให้ถูก

Teacher's Secret Story (Don't let the student read this!):
"Yesterday, I is (was) very tired. I went to the supermarket to buy a (an) apple. But there was (there were) many people! So, I didn't bought (buy) anything."

C. Game: 2 Good, 1 Bad 🎯 เกม: ถูก 2 ผิด 1

Rule: Find the ONE sentence that has bad grammar! กฎ: หาประโยค 1 ประโยคที่ผิดแกรมม่า!
