2. Homework Check 📺📝 ตรวจการบ้านวิดีโอ

Did you watch the YouTube video? Let's check! 🕵️‍♂️
คุณได้ดูวิดีโอ YouTube หรือเปล่า? มาเช็คกันเถอะ! 🕵️‍♂️

1. Which one means you like to perform the action yourself (not just watch)? อันไหนหมายความว่าคุณชอบลงมือทำสิ่งนั้นด้วยตัวเอง (ไม่ใช่แค่ดู)?
• Like + to (เช่น I like to sing)
• Like + V-ing (เช่น I like singing)

Correct! 🎉 "Like + to" is for performing an action yourself, or habits!

2. "I like having a beard." Which rule is this? "I like having a beard." นี่คือกฎข้อไหน?
• A habit (นิสัย)
• A current state/situation (สถานะ/สถานการณ์ปัจจุบัน)

Correct! 🎉 "Like + V-ing" is used for current states and situations.

3. "She likes to dance." Why is there an 's' on 'likes'? "She likes to dance." ทำไมถึงมีตัว 's' ที่คำว่า 'likes'?
• Because of 'She' (เพราะประธานเป็น He/She/It)
• Because dance is plural (เพราะ dance เป็นพหูพจน์)

Correct! 🎉 Present Simple rule: He, She, It + V(s/es).

Jerry: Yes! She likes eating baby back ribs! 🍖
Tom: And yesterday? Did you like the food at the party?

ทอม: ที่นี่คึกคักมากเลย! คุณชอบกินข้าวผัดไหม?
เจอร์รี่: ไม่ ฉันไม่ชอบกินข้าวผัด ฉันเป็นมังสวิรัติ

เจอร์รี่: ใช่! เธอชอบกินซี่โครงหมู!

4. Grammar Rules Recap 📏 สรุปกฎไวยากรณ์

✅ Like + to (Infinitive)

1. To perform an action (เน้นการลงมือทำด้วยตัวเอง):
"I like to sing." (ฉันชอบร้องเพลงเอง ไม่ใช่แค่ดูคนอื่นร้อง)

2. Habits, customs, and preferences (นิสัย, ธรรมเนียม, สิ่งที่ชอบทำประจำ):
"In China, they like to eat with chopsticks." (ในจีน พวกเขาชอบกินด้วยตะเกียบ)
"I like to exercise every morning." (ฉันชอบออกกำลังกายทุกเช้า)

✅ Like + V-ing

1. Action in general (เน้นภาพรวม/สิ่งนั้นๆ ทั่วไป):
"I like skating." (ฉันชอบสเก็ต อาจจะชอบแค่ดูแต่ไม่ได้เล่นเอง)

2. Current state and situations (สถานะหรือสถานการณ์ที่เป็นอยู่ปัจจุบัน):
"I like having a beard." (ฉันชอบที่ตัวเองมีหนวด)
"I like living in the US." (ฉันชอบการใช้ชีวิตในอเมริกา)

📝 Subject Rules

I / You / We / They + like

He / She / It + likes

❌ Negative & ❓ Question

Don't like / Doesn't like + to/V-ing
(He doesn't like swimming.)

Do / Does ... like ... ?
(Do you like to dance?)

Past (อดีต): Didn't like / Did you like...?
(I didn't like the movie yesterday.)

5. Detective Exercises 🕵️‍♂️📝 แบบฝึกหัดนักสืบ

A. Multiple Choice

1. She ________ watching horror movies. 👻

Correct! "She" needs "likes".

2. I don't like ________ to the dentist. 🦷

Correct! like + V-ing -> "like going". (Or "like to go")

3. ________ your father like to drink coffee? ☕

Correct! "Your father" = He -> Does he like...?

B. Error Correction 🔧

Find the mistake and click "Show Fix"!

❌ 1. He didn't liked the fried rice yesterday.

✅ He didn't like the fried rice yesterday.
(After didn't, use base verb V1)

❌ 2. Do they like to sleeping late?

✅ Do they like to sleep late? OR Do they like sleeping late?

C. Jumbled Sentences 🧩

Make a correct sentence in your head, then check!

[ to / like / read / you / Do / books ? ]

✅ Do you like to read books? 📚

[ doesn't / cleaning / She / like / the / wardrobe . ]

✅ She doesn't like cleaning the wardrobe. 👗

6. Funny Speaking Practice 🗣️🎙️ ฝึกพูดแบบตลกๆ

A. Role Play: The Picky Customer 😤🍽️

Waiter: Do you like eating meat? 🥩
Customer (You): No, I ________.
Waiter: Do you like to eat vegetables? 🥦
Customer (You): No, I ________.
Waiter: What DO you like eating?!
Customer (You): I like eating ________! (Say something weird, e.g., cellphones, shoes, A/C!)

B. Funny Interview: Past vs Present ⏳👶

Ask your partner/teacher:

  • Did you like eating vegetables when you were a baby? 🥦👶
  • Do you like playing with dolls now? 🧸
  • What did you like to watch on TV last year? 📺

C. Debate: Morning vs Night 🌅🦉

Team A: I like getting up early! Because...
Team B: I like sleeping late! Because...

Use past vocab: early bird, owl, noisy, quiet, dark, sun.

D. Storytelling: The Clean Ghost 👻🧹

Tell a story using: There is, Present Continuous, Like + V-ing

"There is a ghost in my house. Right now, he is washing dishes! 🍽️🧼
He likes tidying up the bedroom. He likes making the bed... 🛏️"

E. Presentation: My Weird Hobbies 🤪🎤

Tell the class 3 things you LIKE doing, and 1 thing you DON'T like doing.

"Hello! I like smelling shoes. 👟👃
I like pretending to be a dog. 🐕
I like grabbing bugs. 🕷️
But... I don't like taking a shower! 🚿❌"

7. Extension Activities ⏱️🕹️ กิจกรรมเสริม (เผื่อเวลาเหลือ)

Game: 2 Truths and 1 Lie (Like Edition) 🤥

Rule: Say 3 sentences about what you like/don't like. 2 are true, 1 is a lie. The teacher must guess the lie!

Example:
1. I like eating raw garlic. 🧄
2. I don't like watching horror movies. 👻
3. I like to sing in the shower. 🚿🎤

Teacher: Number 1 is a lie!

Game: Rapid Fire Questions! 🔥🗣️

Rule: The teacher asks "Do you like to...?" very fast. You must answer "Yes, I do" or "No, I don't" immediately!

- Do you like to wash dishes?
- Do you like eating raw fish?
- Do you like to pick up trash?
- Do you like sleeping on the floor?
