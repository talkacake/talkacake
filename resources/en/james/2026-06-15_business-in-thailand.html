Cultural Insights: Thai Business Etiquette

文化洞察：泰国商务礼仪
🤝
Before we start our role-play, I have a few quick tips about Thai business culture. This will help make our conversations smoother.
在我们开始角色扮演之前，我有几个关于泰国商业文化的小提示。这将有助于让我们的交谈更顺畅。
😊
Building Relationships: Thai people value personal relationships. A little bit of small talk before getting to business is always a good idea.
建立关系：泰国人看重人际关系。在谈论业务之前进行一些简短的寒暄总是个好主意。
🙏
Politeness: Using polite language and smiling is very important. Saying 'Sawasdee krap' (for men) and giving a 'wai' (Thai greeting) makes a great first impression.
礼貌：使用礼貌用语和保持微笑非常重要。说一句“Sawasdee krap”（男性用语）并做一个“wai”（泰式双手合十礼）会给人留下很好的第一印象。
💳
Business Cards: You should give and receive business cards with your right hand, or both hands, to show respect. It's also polite to look at the card before putting it away.
名片：你应该用右手或双手递交和接收名片，以示尊重。在收起名片前先看一眼也是礼貌的表现。
