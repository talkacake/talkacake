👀 Baby Steps: Reading Thai!

You've seen these shapes a lot today. Let's learn 1 vowel and 1 consonant by just looking at the shapes!

า

Sara Aa (Vowel)

Makes the long "aa" sound (like car).

Look for it in: จาน (jaan) 🍽️

ง

Ngo Ngu (Consonant)

Makes the "ng" sound (like sing).

Look for it in: ล้าง (láang) 🧼

🗣️ Real-Life Speaking Time!

Let's practice these words in real situations.

🎭 Role-Play: At the Restaurant

Scenario: You just finished eating a big meal in Bangkok.

Action: Tell the teacher you are full, but you still want dessert.

Keywords to use: อิ่ม (ìm) 🤰, ของหวาน (kɔ̌ɔng wǎan) 🍰

💬 Mini-Debate / Discussion

Topic: Are tattoos strange or cool? 🖋️

Action: Express your very simple opinion.

Keywords to use: ช่างสัก (châang sàk) 🧑‍🎨, แปลก (plàek) 👽, ใหม่ (mài) 🆕

🎤 Quick Interview

Action: The teacher will ask you: "Have you ever been to China?"

Keywords to listen for/use: จีน (jeen) 🇨🇳, ทุกอย่าง (tóok-yàang) 🌍
