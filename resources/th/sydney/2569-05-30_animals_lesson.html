Task 1: Your Pets. Tell me about pets you have, or pets you had when you were young. If you've never had a pet, tell me about a friend's pet.

Task 2: Design a Pet. Imagine you can have any pet in the world (real or fantasy). What would it be? What is it like? Use the new vocabulary.

Part A: Can you find the '-aa' sound (สระ อา)? Look for this shape: า. Let's find it in these words: หมา (mǎa), ปลา (bplaa), อ่าน (àan), ขาย (khǎai), ล้างจาน (láang jaan).
