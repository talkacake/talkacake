- คุณเหนื่อยใช่ไหมคะ

Vocabulary: เรียก (rîak) = to call
- A friend is calling.
- Mom is calling.
Vocabulary: อย่างนั้น (yàang-nán) = like that, in that way
- Do it like that.

Structure: ...ใช่ไหม (...châi-mǎi) = ..., right?
