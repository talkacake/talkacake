คนขาย: ได้ค่ะ ลดให้เหลือ 450 บาทค่ะ
ลูกค้า: โอเคค่ะ ___________เงินสดค่ะ

lûuk-káa: kɔ̌ɔ lɔɔng sái M dâi mái ká ... (lɔɔng sèt láaew) ... sài dâi pɔɔ-dii ləəi kâ ______ mái ká
kon kăai: dâi kâ lót hâi lʉ̌a sìi-rói hâa-sìp bàat kâ
lûuk-káa: oo-kee kâ jàai ngən-sòt kâ
Customer: Excuse me, how much are these pants?
Seller: 500 baht.
Customer: Do you have other sizes?
Seller: We have sizes M and L.
Customer: Can I try on size M? ... (After trying on) ... It fits perfectly! Can I get a discount?
Seller: Yes, I can reduce it to 450 baht.
Customer: Okay, I'll pay with cash.
