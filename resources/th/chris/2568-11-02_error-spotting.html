Idea 1: Role-Play Flip

You (the teacher) pretend to be the student, and the student becomes the teacher. The student must ask you the questions:

  • "คุณสบายดีไหม" (khun sà baai dii mái)
  • "คุณชื่ออะไร" (khun chûe à-rai)
  • "คุณชอบ...ไหม" (khun chɔ̂ɔp ... mái)
  • "คุณหิวไหม" (khun hǐw mái)

Idea 2: "My Favorites"

The student tries to make sentences about their real preferences. You can help them build the sentences.

  • Prompt: "What do you like to eat?" (Student might say "ไก่")
    Build: "ผมชอบไก่" (phǒm chɔ̂ɔp gài)
  • Prompt: "What do you like to watch?" (Student might say "เอเลี่ยนเอิร์ธ")
    Build: "ผมชอบดูซีรีส์เอเลี่ยนเอิร์ธ" (phǒm chɔ̂ɔp duu sii-rîi ee lîan ə́ət)

Idea 3: Creative (Silly) Sentences

Try to make funny or strange sentences using the words you know. This is a great way to test the limits of the vocabulary!

  • "ไก่ไม่สบาย" (gài mâi sà baai) - The chicken is not comfortable.
  • "โซฟาอร่อย" (soo faa à-rɔ̀ɔi) - The sofa is delicious.
  • "ผมชอบสอนปลา" (phǒm chɔ̂ɔp sŏon bplaa) - I like to teach fish.
