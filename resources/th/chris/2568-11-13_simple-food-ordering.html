Activity 3: Food & Numbers

Let's learn new words! Practice these questions with your teacher.

  • Food: "คุณชอบกินอะไร"
    khun chɔ̂ɔp gin à-rai What do you like to eat?
  • Thai Food: "คุณรู้ชื่ออาหารไทยอะไร"
    khun róo chûe aa-hǎan tai à-rai What Thai food names do you know?
  • Drinks: "แล้วน้ำล่ะ... ชอบกินน้ำอะไร"
    láew náam lâ... chɔ̂ɔp gin náam à-rai What about drinks... What drinks do you like?
  • Numbers: "คุณรู้ตัวเลขภาษาไทยไหม"
    khun róo dtua-lêk paa-sǎa tai mái Do you know Thai numbers?

Activity 4: Let's Practice!

Idea 1: Role-Play (Ordering Food)

Let's practice ordering food. Try this simple role-play!

  • Customer:
    "ส้มตำ เท่าไหร่คะ"
    sôm dtam tâo rài khá Som Tum, how much?
  • Server:
    "(ห้าสิบ) บาทค่ะ"
    (hâa sìp) bàat kà (50) Baht.

  • Server:
    "เอาอะไรคะ"
    ao à-rai khá What would you like?
  • Customer:
    "ปลาทอด หนึ่ง"
    bplaa thâwt nìng Fried fish, one.

Idea 2: Talk About Interests (Food, Music, Films)

Let's talk about your interests. Practice with `ชอบ` (chɔ̂ɔp), `ไม่ชอบ` (mâi chɔ̂ɔp), and `...ไหม` (mái).

  • "คุณชอบกินไก่ไหม"
    khun chɔ̂ɔp gin gài mái Do you like to eat chicken?
  • "คุณชอบฟังเพลงอะไร"
    khun chɔ̂ɔp fang pleeng à-rai What music do you like to listen to?
  • "ซีรีส์ 'เอเลี่ยนเอิร์ธ' ดีไหม"
    sii-rîi 'ee lîan ə́ət' dii mái Is the series 'Alien Earth' good?

Idea 3: Creative (Silly) Sentences

Check understanding by making funny sentences!

  • "ไก่ไม่สบาย"
    gài mâi sà baai The chicken is not comfortable.
  • "โซฟาอร่อย"
    soo faa à-rɔ̀ɔi The sofa is delicious.
  • "ไข่ดาวพูดภาษาอังกฤษ"
    kài daao pûut paa-sǎa ang-grìt The fried egg speaks English.
